Starting soon, my posts will first be posted to this website before being syndicated to [other platforms](/verify).
This is a strategy known as [POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE).
> Publish (on your) Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere, the practice of posting content on your own site first, then publishing copies or sharing links to third parties (like social media silos) with original post links to provide viewers a path to directly interacting with your content.

Posts published after the implementation of this policy will contain a backlink to the original page, likely to be something like `/social/[timestamp]`.

For more information on POSSE, check out [Molly White's article](https://www.citationneeded.news/posse/).
