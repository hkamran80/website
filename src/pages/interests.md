## Public transit

I love public transit!
I would happily take transit any day of the week, provided it wasn't significantly longer than driving.
My favourite transit app is [Transit](https://transitapp.com).

I'm [currently building](/now#projects) a GTFS-powered transit log/dashboard to track all my trips.

## Open data / standards

I like open data and open standards, something that is combined with my love of public transit because of the [General Transit Feed Specification](https://gtfs.org) (GTFS), an open standard for transit services.
