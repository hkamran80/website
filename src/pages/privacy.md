This policy applies to this website, hkamran.com, and my other sites.

## Information Collected

I use a self-hosted [Umami](https://umami.is) instance to analyze which pages are most accessed.
To quote [their documentation](https://umami.is/docs):
> No cookies, no tracking across sites, no personal data collection. GDPR compliant out of the box.

These analytics are stored on my server in the United States and are used to see where my traffic is coming from.
Only I have access to the data.

## Changes

I may change this policy at any time to reflect updates to my processes, current acceptable practices, or legislative or regulatory changes.
If I decide to change this privacy policy, I will post the changes here at the same link by which you are accessing this privacy policy.

If required by law, I will get your permission or give you the opportunity to opt in to or opt out of, as applicable, any new uses of your personal information.
