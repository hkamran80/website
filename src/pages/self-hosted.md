- [**AirTrail**](https://airtrail.johan.ohly.dk)

  I love visualizations and tracking my own data.
  This is the best service for tracking all your past flights.

- [**Authentik**](https://goauthentik.io)

  SSO is a must in my opinion, especially when you host many services like I do.
  I've looked into other systems like Authelia and Pocket ID, but haven't considered switching yet.

- [**FreshRSS**](https://freshrss.org)

  The best feed aggregator that I have found.
  I use the web interface when I'm on my laptop and [Read You](https://github.com/ReadYouApp/ReadYou) when I'm on my phone.

- [**Healthchecks**](https://healthchecks.io)

  A simple heartbeat monitor.

- [**Home Assistant**](https://www.home-assistant.io)

  Home Assistant is the best smart home platform, aside from the [lack of support for SSO](https://github.com/orgs/home-assistant/discussions/48).

- [**Jellyfin**](https://jellyfin.org)

  The best media server in my opinion.
  The Jellyfin team has been doing some amazing work!

  On my laptop, I use [Jellyfin Desktop](https://github.com/jellyfin/jellyfin-desktop).
  On my phone, I use [Findroid](https://github.com/jarnedemeulemeester/findroid) for watching media and the official [Jellyfin app](https://github.com/jellyfin/jellyfin-android) or [Streamyfin](https://github.com/streamyfin/streamyfin/) for Quick Connect.

- [**linkding**](https://linkding.link)

  My bookmarks and read it later system.

- [**Paperless-ngx**](https://docs.paperless-ngx.com)

  The best document manager.

- [**Sure**](https://sure.am)

  A personal finance app.

- [**Umami**](https://umami.is)

  A great privacy-focused analytics system that has "no cookies, no tracking across sites, no personal data collection", and is "GDPR compliant out of the box".

- [**Zigbee2MQTT**](https://www.zigbee2mqtt.io)

  The way I integrate my Zigbee network into Home Assistant.

- [**Z-Wave JS**](https://zwave-js.github.io/zwave-js-ui)

  The way I integrate my Z-Wave network into Home Assistant.
